    Hi I’m Otrek, an engineer, software developer, and project manager, who loves to create solutions, learn new things, and is always open to a good challenge. For more than 14 years I’m always involved in some kind of software development project related to the analysis and management of data.

    I started my career as a vehicle engineering student at TU-Berlin but realized that HPC Software development is my go-to task pretty quick. Since 2007 I am developing numerical software and software tools for virtual vehicle engineering. Mainly in Python, C/C++ but also Fortran and various other languages.

    In 2015 I started my journey towards project management, first with classic methods according to IPMA. As I was creating software together with my team, we soon became aware that these are not the right tools for a software project, therefore, I started digging into agile methods like Scrum and Kanban. As of now, I’m a certified Scrum Master and Product Owner and experienced in growing agile methods and mindsets in inexperienced teams.

    Though I have been working with databases for quite some time, I got heavily involved in data warehousing and data engineering starting in 2018. Here I quickly realized that classic data warehousing is suboptimal for a digital company, generating knowledge from their data. Though I don’t want to make a case against data warehousing, to implement the digital representation of knowledge for an intelligent organization, it is key to go beyond.

    The Data Logistics Platform is a concept that helps companies to understand their data and connect their services and solutions to create intelligent decisions based on data.
