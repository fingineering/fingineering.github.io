Data Engineering

Do you know that feeling, when you have to manually copy information from one system to another? A Data Logistics Platform can make that go away! Enter data once, generate Information everywhere.

You do have data, loads of data! You just need to access it!

What to expect when working with me

If you know the Why let’s start with the Where!

First, we investigate where your data is, or start with answering your Why!

Together, we discuss which sources to connect and design an architecture that can be extended as you create inside and think of new questions.

We will not only discuss where to find data but also, where this data might create additional value!

During the engineering phase, I‘ll connect your source systems as needed and build the infrastructure in the cloud. As every company is a software company, everything is software. Therefore, you will get your new infrastructure defined as code and ready to grow.

Single Data Engineering tasks

Maybe, you already have data and a data logistics platform, but that set of data you found somewhere would increase the informational value but it doesn’t fit. If it is accessible it can fit, we can construct transformation to integrate it.

The Data Logistics Platform

If you ever thought about your data in your business, you have heard about BI (Business Intelligence). This is the layer you see when you are looking at your data. Data engineering enables BI.

But, there is more to do with data. Getting the data your business creates together enables you to venture into the realm of intelligent organization.

Using the idea of the Data Logisitics Platform leads to an even more powerful platform within your environment. A Data Logisitics Platform connects the systems and services you use on the data level. Therefore, information that is created in one system is available as the same information in another system. Workflows get connected with each other on the data level, software and system start to represent the digital twin of your company

On-Premise or in the Cloud?

Basically, it is up to you, though I must admit, using the power of cloud tools, whether it is on Azure, AWS or Google Cloud Platform will be cheaper and easier.

Let’s get in touch and see how we can create a Data Logisitics Platform for your specific needs. Starting with understanding your requirements, mining your data, and creating a platform to connect your systems and services.
